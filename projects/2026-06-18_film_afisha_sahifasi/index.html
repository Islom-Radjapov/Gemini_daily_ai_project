Sci-Fi, Drama, Adventure | 2014 | 2h 49m | PG-13

Directed by: Christopher Nolan

Synopsis

In a dystopian future where humanity is struggling to survive, a group of explorers travel through a newly discovered wormhole near Saturn in an attempt to find a new habitable planet for mankind. Led by pilot Cooper, the team embarks on a perilous journey beyond our galaxy, facing unimaginable challenges and the relentless ticking of time.
